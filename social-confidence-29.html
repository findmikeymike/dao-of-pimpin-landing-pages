There was Somewhere in him that said maybe it wasn’t over… Maybe hidden under piles of Barbara Streisand vinyl and weary old lamp shades, in some dusty storage room of that pawn shop on Dunlevy Avenue, the tasseled Harley jacket would still be there, waiting for him.

Master, how did you come to understand this truth?

Master, what separates those who master this from those who don't?

In the back of the armored police van, the Master, Raul, and Gregory sat in complete silence. The Master had not lifted his head since his arrest. As he sniffled, he continued to sing quietly. Gregory and Raul glanced at each other, their faces blank and expressionless.
